EXHIBIT Arhitectura was founded in 2007. Our team has designed and accompanied throughout the execution a wide range of constructions and developments for clients such as: Quin Auto¬motive, Total Romania, Electroprecizia Logistic Park, GlaxoSmithKline / Europharm, Brasov Maternity, INAR, Alpla Romania / Star East Pet, Brasov City hall, Velux Romania, Temad , Eldon, Auchan, LBBW Immobilien/Dootzdevelopment, Humanitas Bookstores Retail

Our main field of activity is architecture design and the projects we have undertaken cover areas of specialty: new construc¬tions, interior design, exterior design / public spaces, restoration / reconversion, object design. Depending on the specific theme of the project and the client’s wish we collaborate with engineering companies or specialists and coordinate all the specialised design engineers involved (structure, installations etc.) to provide complete general design services.
Our projects have been equally appreciated by clients, the end user, as well as the professional environment (the awards and publications our works have been mentioned in bear witness).
